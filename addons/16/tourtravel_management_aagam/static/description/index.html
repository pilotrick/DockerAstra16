Tour, Travel and Hotel booking management in odoo

Tour Booking, Booking Service, Tour Agents, Hotel Booking in odoo

Tour and Travel management odoo module helps to manage contracts with all your suppliers with your accommodation, transportation, restaurants, guides and other logistical requirements.

You can configure accommodation prices based on date range pricing for the packages can be set according to the currency and can be calculated as per the exchange rate of the selected currency. With tour management odoo module, tour operators can also calculate cost per person for adult and child and Tour agent or user can define sales price and cost price to maintain a margin of travel packages in odoo.

Tour Reservation in odoo, Travel Booking in odoo, create invoice for booking, hotel booking in odoo. Also we have implemented functionality for Agent registration, commission, import data, We will provide complete configuration for roles & rights and other features added for agents, tour consultants, drivers.

Tour Travel Management module Supported in Odoo Community and Odoo Enterprise

Tour and travel management in odoo module Supported Odoo V16, V15, V14, V13, V12 version with Multiple languages supported (English, Arabic, Chinese Traditional, Chinese Simplified, Danish-dansk, Dutch-Nederlands, Spanish - Español, French-français) in odoo backend.
